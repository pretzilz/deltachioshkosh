The University of Wisconsin Oshkosh has had Delta Chi on their campus two times before our colony. This time we are here to stay, as our Founders' Shirts say we are here to "Redefine the college social fraternity." We are here to not only uphold our founding values, but to raise our fraternity to new heights. This does not come easily and we are determined to work hard and become a full fledged Delta Chi Chapter. We hope you will join us in that misson.
