The Delta Chi Fraternity was founded on October 13th, 1890 at Cornell University. Ever since then the men who have joined our numbers have sworn to uphold these four values:
  • Education
  • Justice
  • Character
  • Friendship
These principles have led to an organization of whoms members pride themselves on being gentlemen and striving for justice in all aspects of life. From those humble beginnings at Cornell arose an organization that has 121 chapters and is expanding every day.
Want to know more about us? Check out the Delta Chi International Website for more information!
These men are the Founding Fathers of the Delta Chi Fraternity, along with their Spiritual Founder Sir Edward Cook.
